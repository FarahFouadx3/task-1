WhatsApp

WhatsApp from Meta is a FREE messaging and video calling app. It’s used by over 2B people in more than 180 countries.
It’s simple, reliable, and private, so you can easily keep in touch with your friends and family.
WhatsApp works across mobile and desktop even on slow connections.

WhatsApp has many features including:
  • Text messaging
  • Audio calls
  • Video calls
  • End-to-end encryption
